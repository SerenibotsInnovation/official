LUNA

An AI wearable device & application that can predict and mitigate Cardiovascular disease with 96% accuracy even during movement.

LUNA Band

AI-Powered Wearable for Real-Time Health Monitoring

An AI-powered wearable that tracks 12 vital signs with 96% accuracy even on the move.
It detects cardiovascular risks in real time, sends emergency alerts, and syncs with the LUNA app for personalized health insights and continuous care.

LUNA App

Your AI-Powered CVD Companion

The LUNA App is your intelligent cardiovascular care partner designed to work with the LUNA Band for real-time risk prediction, and proactive management of heart health.

It analyzes vital signs, habits, and medical history to deliver personalized alerts, lifestyle guidance, and emergency support, all through one smart, mobile platform.
